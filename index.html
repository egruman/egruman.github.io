Past and Current Projects

  • Behavioral Evolution Simulator- Neural Evolution Simulation

    Behavioral evolution simulate an environment in which natural selection is favored. Creatures are started with random behavioral traits (as genetic information) which can mutate as it reproduces. Those most successful at survival/ resource procurement will reproduce more frequently and so population will evolve to have the most beneficial behavioral traits. Behavioral traits are represented as a string of characters, which each stand for a specific condition (lower case means negation of that condition). They are then followed by a number which stands for the action to be taken, given that all the conditions are met.
